When Season 2 (Light and Darkness) launched, my wife spent ages hunting for Priest guides. Good strategy posts on the community boards were buried between questions and memes, so picking out just the useful information was harder than it should be. To help her out, I collected the Priest posts and organized them into a proper guidebook — and when my guildmates in the Friends guild (Lasa server) saw it and asked for their classes too, it somehow grew into a **complete edition covering every class**.

The current version compiles **around 200 posts and comments** from the Evan (Mabinogi Mobile) community gallery, posted between the Season 2 launch (June 25) and July 5, into one guidebook per class — **21 classes in total**. Going forward, it will **automatically re-collect and refresh every week**. The revision date is shown at the bottom of each book.

- **Melee**: Swordmaster · Greatsword · Knight (new) · Sword & Shield · Rogue · Dual Blade · Fighter
- **Ranged**: Archer · Crossbow · Longbow
- **Magic**: Mage · Fire Mage · Ice Mage · Lightning Mage · Dark Mage
- **Support**: Priest · Healer · Monk · Bard · Musician · Dancer

Every book follows the same structure: an **at-a-glance summary** (tier, build, reforge, and stat targets) at the top, followed by chapters on skill rotations, reforge/stat tables, and rune setups, ending with a **source list**. Every claim is annotated with the original post number, so one click takes you to the source.

You can browse the library at [**Tools → Mabinogi Mobile S2 Guide Library**](/tools/mabi-guide/). Click a class cover to open its guidebook. There is also an **all-in-one single file** (all 21 books plus the report, about 0.6 MB) available for offline keeping at the bottom of the library — but since downloads are frozen at the moment you save them, **I recommend reading on the blog for the weekly-updated latest version**. (Content is in Korean.)

If it helps my wife's Priest — and my guildmates' characters — get a little stronger, that's all I need.

> This guide library is an unofficial compilation of the collective knowledge of the Evan gallery community. My thanks to every user who wrote the original posts.
